Senior Citizens Savings Scheme (SCSS)

What is the Senior Citizens Savings Scheme (SCSS)?

The Senior Citizens Savings Scheme (SCSS) is a government-backed savings program designed to provide financial security to senior citizens aged 60 years and above. The scheme offers regular income along with tax benefits, making it a safe investment option for elderly individuals.

Key Features

  • Eligibility: Individuals aged 60 years and above. For individuals between 55–60 years, the scheme is available if they have voluntarily retired under a government scheme.
  • Interest Rate: Currently, the SCSS offers an attractive interest rate of 8% per annum, paid quarterly.
  • Investment Amount: The minimum investment is ₹1,000, and the maximum is ₹15 lakh (either individually or jointly).
  • Tenure: The scheme comes with a tenure of 5 years, which can be extended for an additional 3 years.
  • Tax Benefits: Contributions qualify for tax deductions under Section 80C of the Income Tax Act. Interest earned is also taxable.

How to Invest

Investing in the SCSS is simple. Follow these steps:

  1. Visit the nearest post office or authorized bank branch.
  2. Fill out the application form for SCSS.
  3. Submit your identification documents (Aadhaar, PAN, etc.).
  4. Make the investment by cash, cheque, or demand draft.
  5. Once your application is processed, you will receive your passbook and investment certificate.
