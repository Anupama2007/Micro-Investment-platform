PMJDY

Pradhan Mantri Jan Dhan Yojana

The Pradhan Mantri Jan Dhan Yojana (PMJDY) is a national mission aimed at ensuring access to financial services such as basic banking accounts, credit, insurance, and pension to the unbanked population of India.

Key Features of PMJDY

  • Zero balance account: You can open a bank account with zero balance.
  • Accident insurance: ₹1 lakh accidental insurance cover for those who open an account.
  • Overdraft facility: Overdraft facility of ₹10,000 after 6 months of account operation.
  • Direct Benefit Transfers (DBT): Benefits of government schemes can be directly transferred to the account.
  • Rupay Debit Card: Free Rupay Debit card for every account holder.

Eligibility

PMJDY is open to all Indian citizens. The only requirements are:

  • Must be a resident of India.
  • Must be at least 18 years of age.
  • Can be a part of a family or an individual.

How to Apply

You can apply for PMJDY at any authorized bank or financial institution. You need to bring the following documents:

  • Proof of Identity (Aadhaar card, Voter ID, PAN, Passport, etc.)
  • Proof of Address (Aadhaar card, utility bills, etc.)
