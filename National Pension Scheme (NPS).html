National Pension Scheme (NPS)

Secure Your Future with NPS: A Government-backed Retirement Savings Scheme

What is NPS?

The National Pension Scheme (NPS) is a long-term retirement savings scheme launched by the Government of India to provide income security to individuals in their retirement years. It is open to all citizens between the age of 18 to 65 years.

Key Benefits of NPS

  • Tax Benefits: NPS offers tax deductions under section 80C and 80CCD.
  • Flexible Investment: You can choose your own investment option â€“ equity, government bonds, or corporate bonds.
  • Low-Cost: NPS has one of the lowest expense ratios among investment options.
  • Transparency: NPS offers high levels of transparency in its management and operations.
  • Portable: You can transfer your NPS account from one job to another, or from one bank to another.

How Does NPS Work?

Under NPS, you open a pension account with an authorized NPS service provider, where you contribute a certain amount regularly during your working years. The accumulated corpus is then used to buy annuity plans at the time of retirement, ensuring a steady income.

Steps to Enroll in NPS

  1. Visit an NPS Enrollment Center or Register Online.
  2. Fill out the application form and provide your KYC documents.
  3. Choose your contribution amount and investment option (Equity, Bonds, or Government Securities).
  4. Start contributing regularly towards your NPS account.
